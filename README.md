<div class="recipe">
    <h2>üçã Lemon Cheesecake</h2>
    <p><strong>Ingredients:</strong></p>
    <ul>
        <li>200g biscuits</li>
        <li>100g butter</li>
        <li>300g cream cheese</li>
        <li>1 cup sugar</li>
        <li>2 lemons (juice)</li>
        <li>Whipping cream</li>
    </ul>
    <p><strong>Steps:</strong></p>
    <ol>
        <li>Crush biscuits and mix with melted butter</li>
        <li>Press mixture into a cake pan</li>
        <li>Mix cream cheese, sugar, and lemon juice</li>
        <li>Add whipping cream and mix well</li>
        <li>Pour over base and chill for 4 hours</li>
    </ol>
